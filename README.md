# Tata Group Data Visualization Virtual Internship

## 📌 Project Overview
This repository contains the work completed during the Tata Group Data Visualization Virtual Experience. The project involved acting as a data consultant to provide the CEO and CMO of a retail business with actionable insights through interactive Power BI dashboards.

## 🏢 Business Scenarios & Task Highlights
The internship was structured around four key consulting tasks:
1. **Framing Business Questions:** Identifying the right metrics for executive decision-making.
2. **Data Cleaning & Selection:** Preparing the online retail dataset by removing invalid entries (negative quantities/prices) and ensuring data integrity.
3. **Data Visualization:** Creating high-impact visuals to answer strategic questions.
4. **Executive Presentation:** Delivering a concise summary of findings for C-suite stakeholders.

## 📊 Dashboard Key Insights
The "E-Commerce Performance Tracker" answers the following critical business questions:
* **Revenue Trends:** Analysis of monthly revenue to identify seasonal peaks and troughs.
* **Global Market Share:** Identification of the top 10 countries by revenue and quantity sold (excluding the UK).
* **Customer Loyalty:** Highlighting the top 10 customers by revenue to drive retention strategies.
* **Product Demand:** Mapping the global footprint to understand regional market concentration.
